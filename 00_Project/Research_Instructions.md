# Instructions for researching this repository

This repository contains my personal research notes, page summaries,
interpretations, and provisional ontology for the project:

*Toward a Fidelity to God: Seizing the Means of Salvation.*

Treat the repository as evidence of my argument and interpretation,
not as an independent scholarly authority.

Distinguish carefully among:

1. claims made explicitly by a primary author;
2. my summaries of those claims;
3. my interpretations;
4. my constructive theological applications;
5. unresolved questions or hypotheses.

Use notes containing textual evidence and page references to locate
the relevant primary argument.

Do not assume that linked concepts are compatible merely because
they appear in the same ontology.

Use external scholarly sources to test:

- whether my readings are textually defensible;
- whether I have overlooked established interpretations;
- whether I am conflating justification, salvation, sanctification,
  reconciliation, and faith;
- whether appropriation can preserve alterity;
- whether my account makes human reception a covert meritorious work;
- whether existing theological categories already accomplish what
  I am attempting to describe.

When discussing Jaeggi, clearly distinguish her own argument from
my theological application of her concepts.
